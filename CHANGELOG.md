# Changelog

## Unreleased

### Changed

- Changed the "Member Solo Picks" to a single "Solo Songs" category.
- Reduced the total number of allowed solo picks from 10 to 3.
- The overall maximum number of selections decreased from 16 to 9.
- Updated the export board to a clean single-page 3-section layout.

### Compatibility

- Older ballots containing up to 10 member solo picks will have their solo picks automatically discarded for fairness. The Group and Project picks will remain intact, and users will be prompted to select their 3 new solo songs.
